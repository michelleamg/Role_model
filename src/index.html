About the Character

Name: Marie Curie

Birth Date: November 7, 1867

Nationality: Polish-French

Profession: Physicist and Chemist

Known For: Research on radioactivity

Childhood

Marie Curie was born in Warsaw, Poland. During her childhood, she was very intelligent and curious. She loved learning and spent a lot of time reading books and studying science.

Young Age

As a young woman, Marie worked hard to continue her studies. She moved to Paris to attend university, where she studied physics and mathematics. Her determination helped her overcome many challenges.

Adult Life

In her adult life, Marie Curie became an important scientist. She worked with her husband, Pierre Curie, and together they made important discoveries. She dedicated her life to science and education.

Achievements

  • Discovered polonium and radium
  • Won two Nobel Prizes
  • Made important contributions to medicine and science
  • Became one of the most influential women in history

Interesting Facts

  • She was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize.
  • She was the first person to win two Nobel Prizes in different sciences.
  • Her research helped develop medical treatments.
